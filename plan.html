What the timing actually does
  • Micro-jumping is the efficiency winner. The 24h booster cap means you eat ~48 lollipops/day however you split them — so jumping every 4h (8 lollipops + Ecstasy each) trains all your energy at doubled happy, not just one bar/day. $245 per extra stat, no Xanax, +7,502 def/wk — the best value here and ~1.5× the output of a single daily lolli jump. Once you're micro-jumping, adding Xanax is poor value (~$3,600/extra-stat to reach Lolli 4-Xan).
  • Chocolate is a trap at every tier. +35 happy for $55,656 vs the lollipop's +25 for $848 — ~47× worse per happy. On the 4-Xan jump, big choco's tiny happy edge buys +1,684 def/wk but costs $13.2M/wk more — about $7,800 per extra stat. Lolli 4-Xan ($864/stat) beats Choco 4-Xan ($1,296) outright.
  • 4-Xan jumps don't gain daily. The sim fires jumps at D2 06:00, D3 16:00, D5 02:00, D6 12:00, D7 22:00 — a 34h cycle, 5/week. Stacking 4 Xanax holds you above 150 energy for ~30h, so ~4,500 energy of regen is lost to banking and Day 1 produces zero.
  • The expensive tiers buy speed, not efficiency. eDvD reaches a stat goal ~5.5× faster than baseline, but at the worst $/stat. Pay for it only when time, not money, is the constraint.
  • Addiction is the hidden tax — and it reshuffles the ranking. Each Xanax = 35 addiction pts, Ecstasy = 20, and you shed only ~140/wk. Lolli daily is the only jump method that's addiction-neutral (120 in < 140 decay). Micro-12h (+120/wk) stays manageable. But the "best value" Lolli micro 4h (+680/wk) and every Xanax method (+660/wk) pile up addiction fast → battle-stat debuff, kicked from education, and a hard gym lockout once total Xanax+Ecstasy used passes 150 — under 4 weeks at micro-4h's pace. Those routines force regular rehab (~$250k each).
